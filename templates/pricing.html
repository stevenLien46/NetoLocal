Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day no-obligation free trial for you to build your store and test out the full-featured Neto platform (no credit card required). After the trial you will be given the option to sign up.

I am looking to switch, is it easy?

To minimise disruption to your business, we have a number of features to make the switch as simple as possible including data import tools for .csv files and a staging environment. You can either do it yourself or you can speak to our team about options available to you through our network of expert partners

Do I need a web host?

No, you don't need a web host. You can either opt for a Neto plan that includes a secure webstore hosted on Netos infrastructure with unlimited storage, bandwidth, and DDoS protection. Alternatively, if you're looking to sell with POS in store or via a marketplace, you don't need a web host

Can I use my own domain name?

Yes, if you have an existing domain name you can use it with Neto. If you don't have a domain name they are available for purchase from domain providers like Crazy Domains or NameCheap.

Neto can host your DNS or you can host your DNS elsewhere and point your domain to your Neto website

* Neto does not charge transaction fees on purchases made on our platform. However, processing credit cards requires a payment gateway (eg. Stripe, your bank, etc), who may charge their own fees such as a percentage of each transaction and/or a small fixed fee.
